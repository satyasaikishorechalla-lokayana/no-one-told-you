Welcome to Mera Adhikar ("the App"), developed and maintained by Lokayana Legal Tech. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

By using Mera Adhikar, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Mera Adhikar is designed with privacy first. We collect minimal to no personal data.

  • No Registration Required: The app does not require you to create an account or provide personal information.
  • No Personal Data Collected: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information.
  • No Location Data: The app does not access or collect your location.
  • Device Information (Anonymous): We may collect anonymous, non-identifiable usage statistics (such as which sections of the app are most viewed) to improve the app. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. How We Use Information

Any anonymous data collected is used solely to:

  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Understand which legal topics are most accessed
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

We do not sell, share, or trade any data with third parties for commercial purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Mera Adhikar uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts (Nunito): Loaded from Google's servers for typography. Google's privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy
  • GitHub Pages: Used to host the app. GitHub's privacy policy applies: GitHub Privacy Statement

We do not use any advertising networks, analytics trackers, or data brokers.

4. Offline Usage & Local Storage

Mera Adhikar is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works offline. When you install it:

  • App files are cached locally on your device for offline use
  • No personal data is stored in this cache — only app code and content
  • You can clear this cache at any time through your browser or device settings

5. Children's Privacy

Mera Adhikar is an educational app suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. The app contains only legal educational content — no harmful, violent, or inappropriate material.

6. Emergency Helplines

The app provides emergency helpline numbers (such as 112, 181, 1930). When you tap these numbers, your phone's dialer app opens. Any call made is handled by your phone carrier — we do not collect or record any call data.

7. Data Security

Since we collect no personal data, there is minimal security risk to users. The app is served over HTTPS (secure connection). All content in the app is static, publicly available legal information.

8. Your Rights

Under Indian law (including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023) and other applicable laws, you have the right to:

  • Know what data is collected about you (answer: none)
  • Request deletion of your data (no data stored)
  • Opt out of data collection (not applicable)

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Content Disclaimer

The legal information provided in Mera Adhikar is for general educational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice. Laws may change over time. For specific legal situations, please consult a qualified lawyer or approach the appropriate legal authority.
